Program

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

The BS Information Technology program includes the study of the utilization of both hardware and software technologies involving planning, installing, customizing, operating, managing and administering, and maintaining information technology infrastructure that provides computing solutions to address the needs of an organization. The program prepares graduates to address various user needs involving the selection, development, application, integration and management of computing technologies within an organization.

(CHED Memo 25: Series 2015)

Mission.

The School of Information Technology shall strive to produce technically competent and socially responsible individuals equipped with knowledge and skills to meet the standards for IT practitioners in the country and global community.

Vision.

The School of Information Technology as the lead in Information Technology education in the region committed to empowering individuals by providing high quality education for the training and development of globally competitive IT professionals and workers in industry, business, government and the academe.
